Common topics

  • Payments — Riders pay by Apple Pay, Google Pay, card or cash; no account needed. Drivers keep 99% of their fare.
  • Availability — Fairgo opens market by market after local review. If it's not live in your area yet, it will say so in the app.
  • Account & data — To access or delete your data, email us from your account address.
  • Cancellations — Cancel free within the grace window; a small fee may apply to late rider cancellations once a driver has committed. Drivers cancel with no penalty.
  • Safety — Use the in-app trip sharing, verify-my-ride PIN, and emergency button. In an emergency, always call your local emergency number first.

For an emergency in progress, contact local emergency services directly. This page is for account and app support.
